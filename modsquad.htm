Join the Mod Squad!
Support, Preserve, and Protect Residential Modernist Architecture

Mod Squad members provide financial support for Modernist Archive, Inc., aka USModernist and NCModernist, an award-winning 501C3 nonprofit educational archive for the documentation, preservation, and promotion of residential Modernist architecture.

The Mod Squad is a thriving community of people dedicated to Modernist residential design. Mod Squad members are into art, food, travel, furniture, fashion, and fun.

Mod Squad membership connects you with like-minded people, building new and meaningful relationships! Mod Squad members support:

  • Groundbreaking research and documentation in the NCModernist and USModernist archives, together the largest open digital archives for Modernist houses

  • Educational initiatives, such as Project BauHow; and awards programs, such as the George Matsumoto Prize for excellence in Modernist residential design

  • Tours of exciting, significant Modernist buildings locally, nationally, and internationally

  • USModernist Radio, where podcast host George Smart talks and laughs with guests about Modernist architecture, the most exciting and controversial buildings in the world

  • Innovative NCModernist event programming, including the Thirst4Architecture Gatherings, the Architecture Movie Series, and other special events

There are two levels of Mod Squad membership, both tax-deductible.

Terrazzo Membership:

For people who want to jump right into our community of Modernist fans!

  • An invitation to the annual ModStruck Gala, with discounts on purchasing guest tickets

  • Special discounts of up to $100 on our wildly popular local, national, and international Modernist tours (when we're doing tours again!)

  • Special advance notice for Modernist tours and other events before the general public

  • nvitation to dynamic social gatherings, curated in venues focusing on architecture, art, culinary arts, and the Modernist lifestyle

  • Opportunity to volunteer for events, waiving or reducing event admission

  • Terrazzo-level membership is only $9.99 a month, and your first month is free! Unsubscribe anytime. Or, you may pay for a year ($120) by making a donation here.

Cantilever Membership:

$300/year - for people committed to sustaining Modernism into the future!

  • All the Terrazzo benefits, plus...

  • A beautiful USModernist pin signifying your support

  • Two free tickets to the annual fall ModStruck Gala with priority for purchasing additional tickets, and special recognition at the Gala

  • Discounts on corporate sponsorship for events, digital publications, programs, and additional initiatives

  • Payment : We accept nearly all credit and debit cards. To make a non-membership donation, go here.
